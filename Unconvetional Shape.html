Unconvetional Shape

This project takes women's body anxiety as the starting point. Through research and understanding, we found that modern society has new standards for "female beauty", and some of them seem to have deviated far from reality. Therefore, this project takes the form of an experience hall, which allows women to experience the different daily routines of "new age beauty" through three major aspects of daily life: clothing, food and housing, thus alleviating women's anxiety about their bodies.
