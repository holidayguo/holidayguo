Bubble Universe

The starting point of this design is the consideration and research of the metabolism theory of architecture with the background of the demolition of the Bank of China's capsule building, and the design of a spatial experience game based on the metabolism theory of world exploration and social collaboration. Through the study of biological cells and structures, the cell form is extracted from them and constitutes the main component of the main building through the use of deformation, reorganization and discrete design techniques, while the "tree", as the main trunk that carries and aggregates the cells, constitutes the main body of the whole building. In order to better reflect the characteristics of the metabolism school, which advocates the growth, change and mobility of architecture and cities, we decided to place the design into the metaverse world.
