Time Wheel

The project concept design based on "Alice in Wonderland" on the basis of the new sequel story settings, and the Mad Hatter will be the main character of the new story, the entire world will also be designed to create the Mad Hatter perspective, as Alice's good friend, the Mad Hatter in the new world will be waiting for Alice's return together with other important characters.
Story setting：Following Alice's successful rescue of the Hatter's family in the Mirror World, the Hatter is finally reunited with his family, and as he and his father spend a long time together, they have increasingly diverg ent ideas about hat-making. He eventually runs away again from the town of Wizzard. Before leaving, he stumbles upon a record of the time cycle at the White Queen's place. As his ideas about hat-making have always been bound by his father, the Mad Hatter finally decides to find a time wheel and create a world of hats in his own time, waiting for Alice's return.
