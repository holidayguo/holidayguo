Sanctuary

Depression is a common mental disorder that has become the fourth most common disease in the world. It is only in recent years that the Chinese public has become aware and knowledgeable about this mental illness due to the exposure of some public figures to the media for phenomena such as suicides caused by depression, and there are relatively few institutions in China for the treatment of depression.
The project provides a place to relieve negative emotions and heal the body and mind for more people who suffer from depression and other mental illnesses. The project introduces the concept of meditation from Eastern culture into the design of the project, and different forms of meditation experience spaces are designed according to the different degrees of depression, and patients can choose different forms of meditation according to their different needs.
