“1+N” City Living Room

“1+N” Service Center is a community designed with the “city living room” concept. The goal of this special design is to serve and facilitate community residents. The tonality is based on the theme of “original color,” offering a place for relaxation through gentle interactions between space and human.

Date: 2017-2018
Discipline: Interior Design
