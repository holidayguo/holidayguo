9 Arts

9 Arts Art Center, located at 23-24 Gillender Street in East London, leverages its proximity to Tech City and renowned artistic and creative districts to create a comprehensive art center fostering a closed-loop digital economy and cultural innovation. Art, as a "recorder" of human events, exists in multiple dimensions. Based on this concept, 9 Arts combines the traditional eight arts with the ninth art. Through visual, auditory, and tactile experiences, it offers a multifaceted artistic journey encompassing exhibitions, interactive experiences, and a blend of both. The goal is to create a platform for artistic creation and exhibition, promoting cultural innovation and artistic development in East London, and providing a multidimensional and multifaceted artistic experience for both artists and art enthusiasts.
