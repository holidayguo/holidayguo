Wuxi Binhu MixC ONE Interior Design

Wuxi Binhu MixC One is a commercial complex that merges the simplicity of an art gallery with dynamic shopping elements. Like galleries, the interiors are clean and minimalist, allowing the lighting to focus on key design elements. We aimed to capture that same spatial feeling and bring it into a shopping mall setting.

Design: Ippolito Fleitz Group - Identity Architects
Location: Wuxi, China
Date: 2021-2023
Discipline: Interior Design
Categroy: Comercial, Shopping Mall
Size: 100k sqm
