Hi !

Yadan Guo, also known as Holiday, is an experienced interior designer who graduated with a Master’s degree in Interior Design from the Royal College of Art (RCA).For her, studying abroad was an opportunity to expand her creative vision and redefine the boundaries of design.

Holiday specializes in commercial and retail space design, with a deep interest in cultural spaces such as exhibitions.She believes design is not only about creating beauty but about enhancing people’s lifestyles through thoughtful spatial planning and atmosphere, bringing more warmth to society. Guided by “Less is More” and “Form Follows Function,” she carefully crafts each detail to balance simplicity and functionality, creating spaces that meet practical needs.

She remains as curious about the world as a newborn and is captivated by the limitless possibilities that design provides. She approaches each project as a work of art, using creativity and innovation to unlock the potential of materials while balancing function and aesthetics. Her “Design as Storytelling” approach allows her to create spaces that are both functional and emotionally engaging.

Having collaborated with esteemed clients on numerous projects, she is open to new challenges and partnerships. Now based in London, Holiday welcomes opportunities in sustainable design and cultural space exploration, bringing spaces to life with a meaningful touch.
